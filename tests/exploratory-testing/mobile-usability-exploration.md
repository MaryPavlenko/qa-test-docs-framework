# Mobile Usability Exploration

## Exploration Goal

Evaluate responsive behavior and mobile usability of Swag Labs.

---

## Areas Explored

- Product catalogue
- Cart interaction
- Checkout form
- Navigation menu

---

## Devices Tested

- iPhone SE
- iPhone 12 Pro
- Galaxy Fold
- iPad Air

---

## Observations

- Cart badge is difficult to notice on smaller screens
- Product titles wrap inconsistently
- Checkout button remains visible without horizontal scrolling

---

## Risks

- Small touch targets may affect usability
- Potential UI overlap on narrow screens

---

## Recommendations

- Increase spacing between cart icon and menu
- Improve responsive text scaling
